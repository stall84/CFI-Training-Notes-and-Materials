## Teaching

1) Teaching describes the use of specialized knowledge to instruct or to train
    - A good instructor should:
        - **Select and organize worthwhile course material**
        - **Lead learners to encode and integrate that material**
        - **Ensure competence in the procedures and methods of a discipline, sustain intellectual curiosity, and promote independent learning**

2) The process of teaching organizes the content to be taught in such a way that the learner can readily understand.
    - An effective instructor engages in the **teaching process** through the following steps:
        - **Preparation**: Establishing learning objectives
        - **Presentation**: Delivering content in a clear and engaging manner.
        - **Application**: Allowing learners to practice and apply the knowledge gain.
        - **Assessment**: Evaluating, understanding and measuring learning outcomes

3) Essential skills required to teach effectively include the following:
    - **People skills**: Effective communication, etc
    - **Subject matter expertise**: Specific levels of expertise with a sincere interest in learning and professional growth
    - **Management skills**: Plan, organize, lead, and supervise
    - **Assessment skills**: Learning behavioral change can be intellectual, attitudinal or physical and overt. That change is assessable.
