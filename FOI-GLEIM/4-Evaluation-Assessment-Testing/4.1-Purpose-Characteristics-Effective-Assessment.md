## Purpose and Characteristics of Effective Assessment

1) No instructor skill **is more important than the ability to _analyze_, _appraise_, and _judge_ learner performance**
    - Assessment is an **essential** and **continuous** component of the teaching and learning process.
        - **Assessment**: Is the process of gathering measurable information to meet evaluation needs. It involves judgement by the instructor and collaboration with the learner during the evaluation stage.
        - Instructors continuously evaluate learner performance in order to provide _guidance_, _suggestions for improvement_, and _positive reinforcement_.
        - To **_enhance a learner's acceptance of further instruction_, the instructor should keep the learner informed of the progress made**
            - This will help **minimize** learner **frustrations**, which will keep the learner motivated to learn.
        - Assessment helps the instructor _see where more emphasis is needed_ and _determine the readiness of the learner to move forward_
    - In order to provide direction and increase learner performance, **assessments must be factual and must be aligned with the completion standards of the lesson**
